Features

Secure Link: Power BI & Odoo with Tokens

Facilitating a secure connection, the Token-based Link seamlessly integrates Power BI and Odoo. Utilizing a unique authentication token from Odoo's "Power BI Direct Connector" alongside a designated URL, users can establish the connection effortlessly. Once configured in Power BI, this secure conduit ensures safe data exchange between the two platforms, enabling comprehensive analysis and reporting capabilities on Odoo's data within Power BI.

Seamless Data Retrieval

Retrieve a comprehensive list of tables within Odoo, including those from custom modules. Select the desired tables to extract data from and proceed by clicking "Load." Customize your data retrieval by applying operations to the selected tables. To initiate the customization process, click "Transform Data."

Effortless Data Refresh: One-Click

With just a single click on the "Refresh" button within Power BI, all modifications made to your Odoo data are seamlessly updated. This streamlined process ensures that your analyses and visualizations in Power BI are always up-to-date with the latest information from Odoo..
